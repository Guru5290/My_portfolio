Robotic WAAM Research

My research combines robotic path planning, process monitoring and control for Wire Arc Additive Manufacturing (WAAM).

Focus areas

  • Robotic toolpath generation for multi-axis WAAM
  • Real-time process monitoring using cameras and thermography
  • Adaptive control strategies to maintain bead quality
  • Integration of sensors and feedback into robot controllers

Selected projects

Adaptive WAAM deposition

Developed a closed-loop system that adjusts deposition parameters based on melt-pool temperature and geometry estimates.

Path optimization for complex geometries

Worked on slicing and path planning methods to reduce defects and minimize overhangs for robotic WAAM cells.
